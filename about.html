ulti-mate history

Why We Created Ulti-Mate

Our inspiration for Ulti-Mate came from our deep passion for Ultimate. Our many years in the game and the wish for automation of game scoring / tracking led us to this path, ultimately. I expect this app to change a lot in the next few years as we listen and respond to feedback from our users. Ultimate players have a history of being amicable, and we intent to show our appreciation through a beautifully designed helper app. Enjoy!

Version Notes

Currently we are in the beta phase for version 1.0. Launch date to be updated soon. Version 1.0 will be launched soon!

What's next?

There are many planned updates coming down the line. To name a few: 1) Creating teams / users / groups. 2) Storing games on a server instead of locally. 3) Organization of pickup / league matches.
